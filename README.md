# Dashboard_Purchase_Shipping_Analysis

# 1. Project Title
# Customer Purchase Behavior and Shipping Analysis 

# 2. Project Problem Statement
# Online retail companies generate huge volumes of customer transaction data every day. However, understanding customer purchasing patterns, product performance,    seasonal demand, customer demographics, and regional sales trends is difficult without proper visualization.

# The company needs a dashboard that can:
      •	Track overall revenue and customer ratings.
      •	Identify high-performing product categories.
      •	Understand customer buying behavior.
      •	Analyze seasonal and monthly sales trends.
      •	Evaluate regional performance.
      •	Study customer demographics such as gender, size preference, and color preference.
      •	Support business decisions for inventory planning, marketing campaigns, and revenue growth.

# 3. Project Objective
 The primary objective of this project is to:
    •	Analyze customer purchase behavior.
    •	Identify top-performing products and categories.
    •	Understand customer preferences.
    •	Monitor sales performance across states.
    •	Track seasonal trends.
    •	Improve inventory and supply chain planning.
    •	Support data-driven business decisions.

# 4. Dataset Overview
    The dataset contains:
      Field	                     Description
      Customer ID:	         Unique customer identifier
      Product Category:	     Accessories, Clothing, Footwear, Outerwear
      Purchase Amount:	     Revenue generated
      Rating:	               Customer satisfaction score
      State:	               Customer location
      Gender:	               Male/Female
      Season:	               Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter
      Payment Method:	       PayPal, Cash, Credit Card, etc.
      Size:	                 S, M, L, XL
      Color:	               Product color
      Date:	                 Purchase date
